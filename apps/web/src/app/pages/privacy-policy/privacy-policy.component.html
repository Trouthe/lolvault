1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information LoL Vault collects, how it is used, and what choices you have. By using LoL Vault, you agree to the data practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information such as email address and sign-in provider.
  • Profile and app data you create, including Riot IDs, board names, and account notes.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device information, and basic diagnostic logs.
  • Billing-related status data for premium access (processed by Paddle).

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve LoL Vault features.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Operate premium access and entitlement checks.
  • Investigate bugs, abuse, and platform reliability issues.
  • Communicate service updates and policy changes.

4. Payments and Billing

Purchases are processed by Paddle. LoL Vault does not store full payment card numbers. Paddle may process billing details according to its own privacy policy.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers that help operate LoL Vault (for example hosting, authentication, analytics, and payment infrastructure), and when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No online platform can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. To request data actions, contact the project team through official LoL Vault support channels.

9. Children

LoL Vault is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us so we can remove it.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy over time. The latest version is always posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact LoL Vault through the official Discord or support channels.
